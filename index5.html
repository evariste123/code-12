about for our Eva-Codes website

Our Visions

  • To be the leading global platform for practical coding education, recognized for launching successful careers in technology.
  • To envision a future where anyone with willpower and curiosity can acquire the tech skills needed to innovate and create their own economic opportunities.
  • To inspire the next generation of creators to not just consume technology, but to build it, shaping industries and empowering people worldwide.
  • To create a world where access to quality computer science education is universal, removing all barriers to entry and ensuring full participation in the digital future.
  • To become the "go-to" resource for lifelong tech learning, continually adapting to new advancements and enriching people's lives through education and innovation.

Our Missions

  • To empower aspiring developers by providing accessible, practical, and up-to-date coding education that translates directly into job-ready skills.
  • To bridge the digital divide by offering inclusive and affordable learning paths, ensuring everyone, regardless of their background, can learn computer science.
  • To deliver high-quality, project-based courses that enable learners to build a strong portfolio and confidently tackle real-world software development challenges.
  • To foster a supportive global community where students and mentors can collaborate, share ideas, and grow together in their coding journeys
  • To constantly innovate our curriculum to keep pace with the rapidly evolving technology landscape, focusing on cutting-edge fields like AI, Web3, and full-stack development.
Our target
  • User Acquisition: Achieve 10,000 active monthly users within the first six months of launch.
  • Course Completion Rate: Maintain an average course completion rate of 75% for all paid courses by the end of the first year.
  • Community Engagement: Grow the online student forum to 5,000 members and facilitate at least 20 mentor-led project groups within 12 months.
  • Job Placement: See 60% of job-seeking course graduates secure a tech-related position within six months of completing the program.
  • Content Expansion: Develop and launch five new expert-level courses in high-demand fields (e.g., AI/ML, Cybersecurity, DevOps) by the end of year two.
